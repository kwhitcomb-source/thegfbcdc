Our Mission

The mission of The Greater First Baptist Church is to love God, to love others, and to magnify the name of Jesus Christ.

Our Vision

The vision of our church is to glorify our God and Savior, Jesus Christ, to make true disciples throughout all the nations by means of missionary activity and support, to minister the ordinances, to edify believers, and to do all that is sovereignly possible and biblically permissible to magnify the name of Jesus.

Meet Our Pastor

This Space is Reserved for the newly elected Pastor

The Greater First Baptist Church has a rich history rooted in faith, education, and service. From its earliest days, the church has emphasized spiritual growth, sound teaching, and a commitment to uplifting the community through worship and outreach.

Over the years, the church has been guided by strong pastoral leadership and supported by a dedicated congregation united around a shared vision. Through prayer, teaching, and service, The Greater First Baptist Church continues to build upon its foundation while embracing growth, renewal, and faithful stewardship for future generations.
