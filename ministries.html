Our Ministries

You Can Make a Difference

Our church is not a place for spectators. It is a place for team members. Whether you are a five-year old child, a ninety-five year old grandmother, a busy mom, or a businessman with big responsibilities, we want you to be a part of our vibrant church community. Here is how you can learn, serve, and grow at this church

👔

Deacon Ministry

Our Deacons serve as spiritual leaders and servants of the church, supporting pastoral care, assisting in worship services, and ministering to the practical and spiritual needs of our congregation.

🏛️

Trustee Ministry

The Trustee Ministry oversees the physical and financial stewardship of our church facilities, ensuring our building and resources are maintained for effective ministry and worship.

👗

Deaconess Ministry

Our Deaconesses provide vital spiritual support and pastoral care, particularly to women and families in the congregation, serving with compassion and dedication.

🕊️

Bereavement Ministry

The Bereavement Ministry walks alongside families during times of loss, providing comfort, support, and practical assistance as they navigate grief and celebrate the lives of their loved ones.

🌟

Chosen Youth & Young Adult Ministry

This ministry empowers young people and young adults to grow in their faith, develop leadership skills, and engage meaningfully with their peers and the broader church community.

🎨

Creative Arts Ministry

The Creative Arts Ministry uses visual arts, drama, media, and other creative expressions to glorify God and enhance worship experiences throughout our church.

👸

Daughters of Zion Women's Ministry

Our Women's Ministry provides fellowship, discipleship, and support for women of all ages, fostering spiritual growth and meaningful connections among the women of our church family.

🌍

Eliza Smallwood Missionary Ministry

Named after one of our founding members, this ministry is dedicated to spreading the Gospel through missionary activity and support, both locally and around the world.

💐

Flower Ministry

The Flower Ministry beautifies our worship space and delivers floral arrangements to members in need, bringing joy and encouragement through the beauty of God's creation.

📰

FOCUS Newsletter Ministry

FOCUS spreads the gospel to our church family and beyond, increases awareness of church activities, and helps acquaint church members with one another through our newsletter.

Learn More
🤝

Hospitality Ministry

Our Hospitality Ministry welcomes visitors and members with warmth and care, creating an atmosphere of Christian love and fellowship in all church gatherings and events.

🙏

Intercessory Prayer Ministry

This ministry is dedicated to lifting up our church, our community, and individual prayer requests before God, believing in the transformative power of prayer.

👨

Men's Ministry

The Men's Ministry encourages men to grow as spiritual leaders in their homes, church, and community through fellowship, discipleship, and service.

🎵

Music Ministry

Our Music Ministry leads the congregation in worship through song, using their musical gifts and talents to glorify God and inspire hearts toward praise and worship.

Music Ministry Interest
🆕

New Members Fellowship Ministry

This ministry welcomes and integrates new members into our church family, helping them find their place and grow in their relationship with Christ and our congregation.

👶

Nursery Ministry

The Nursery Ministry provides loving care for our youngest members during worship services, creating a safe and nurturing environment for infants and toddlers.

⚕️

Nurses Ministry

Our Nurses Ministry provides health awareness, first aid support during services and events, and care for members experiencing medical needs within our church family.

💝

Outreach Ministry

The Outreach Ministry extends the love of Christ beyond our walls through community service, evangelism, and meeting practical needs in our neighborhood and city.

🛡️

Pastor's Aid Ministry

This ministry supports and encourages our pastoral leadership through prayer, practical assistance, and acts of appreciation, helping to lighten the load of ministry.

💎

R.U.T.H. Ministry

The R.U.T.H. Ministry (Reaching Up Through Him) ministers to women through biblical teaching, fellowship, and spiritual encouragement, fostering growth in faith and service.

💃

Sacred Dance Ministry

Our Sacred Dance Ministry worships God through movement and choreography, using dance as a form of worship and expression of praise to our Creator.

🚪

Senior & Young Adults Usher Ministry

The Usher Ministry serves as the welcoming presence of our church, guiding worshippers, maintaining order, and ensuring a smooth and reverent worship experience for all.

📖

Sunday School Ministry

Our Sunday School provides in-depth Bible study and spiritual education for all ages, from Nursery to Adult Classes, helping members grow in their understanding of God's Word.

📚

Sunday School Missionary Ministry

This ministry supports our Sunday School program while also engaging in missionary education and outreach, teaching about God's global mission to make disciples of all nations.

🚌

Transportation Ministry

The Transportation Ministry provides shuttle services and transportation assistance to ensure all members can participate in worship and church activities, removing barriers to fellowship.

⚔️

Warriors for Christ Youth Missionary Ministry

This youth-focused ministry equips young people to be bold witnesses for Christ, engaging in missions, service, and evangelism with courage and faith.

🎂

Birthday Ministries

The Birthday Ministry celebrates members on their special day, recognizing and honoring each person as a beloved child of God and valued member of our church family.

📝

Get Involved

Our church is not for spectators. It is for team members. No matter your age or season of life, you belong here.
