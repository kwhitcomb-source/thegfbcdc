Reverend Robert Hood

Associate Minister

Rev. Robert Hood serves as Associate Minister at The Greater First Baptist Church. Born and raised in Lynchburg, Virginia, he was nurtured at the historic New Vine Baptist Church and saved at a young age.

Education: Bachelor's Degree in Recreational Leadership, University of the District of Columbia (1982); Master's Degree in Biblical Studies, Maple Springs Baptist Bible College and Seminary (2010).

Ministry: Licensed to preach in 2001 and ordained in 2005, Rev. Hood is committed to preaching, teaching, and evangelizing. He has served in various roles including Lay Ministry, Sunday School, Usher Board, and Community Outreach.

Professional Service: Director of Therapeutic Recreation and Acting Chaplain at The Stoddard Baptist Nursing Home, formerly with District of Columbia Special Olympics, Inc.
