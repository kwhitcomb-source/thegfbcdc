Reverend, Doctor, Jonathan Z. Queen

Pastor, The Greater First Baptist Church

Education & Early Ministry

Reverend Doctor Jonathan Z. Queen received his call to be a Minister of the Gospel in March of 2009, while walking as a deacon-elect. He preached his first sermon on August 22, 2009, and was licensed a Minister of the Gospel on that day by First Baptist Church of Lexington, Virginia. In 2010, Minister Jonathan Queen joined the Shiloh Baptist Church of York, Pennsylvania as an Associate Minister and Youth Leader and was ordained Reverend Jonathan Queen on March 30, 2014.

Dr. Queen received his Bachelor of Science degree in Bible and Christian Ministry from Lancaster Bible College in 2011 and received his Master of Divinity Degree from Lancaster Theological Seminary in May of 2015. In March of 2024, Jonathan successfully defended his doctoral dissertation and earned his Doctor of Ministry from Regent University.

Ministry Journey

Today, Dr. Jonathan Queen serves as an Associate Pastor and Department Head Director for the Children and Youth Ministries of the First Baptist Church of Glenarden International under Senior Pastor John K. Jenkins, Sr. Dr. Queen manages a staff of 9 people and hundreds of volunteers, 18 ministries and 5 major programs such as the Children's Sunday School, Girl Scouts, Boy Scouts, Kingdom Kids Summer Camp, the Unashamed Youth Ministry and Remix Sunday which occurs every 3rd Sunday and features the children and youth of the church leading in multiple areas for all 3 services.

Leadership at The Greater First Baptist Church has consistently encouraged the development of ministries that strengthen worship, education, outreach, and service. These efforts have supported spiritual growth and fostered a culture of engagement, compassion, and stewardship.

Leadership & Ministry Excellence

Rev. Dr. Queen is also an award-winning author of 6 books, a motivational speaker, At-Risk youth counselor, facilitator, and expert in the fields of Mentoring and Reentry. Dr. Queen is a graduate of the Skinner Leadership Institute's 'Masters Series for Distinguished Leaders', the Howard University Business School's Advanced Executive Leadership for Ministers and a two-time graduate of the Baylor University's 'Joel Gregory Proclaimers Place' Seminar.

Global and Outreach Ministry

Dr. Queen has done missionary work and proclaimed the gospel throughout the United States and in numerous countries such as South Africa, Rwanda, and Peru.

Church Family

Rev. Dr. Jonathan Queen and Rev. Lenia Queen M.A., NCC, LCPC live in Upper Marlboro, Maryland, and have six adult children and nine grandchildren.
