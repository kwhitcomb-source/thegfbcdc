FOCUS Newsletter

Conceived by Rev. Ridley in 2003

About FOCUS

FOCUS is The Greater First Baptist Church Newsletter Ministry that serves to:

  • Spread the gospel to the immediate church family and beyond.
  • Increase the church family's awareness of church activities, the church's vision, its ministries, and news.
  • Acquaint church members with one another.

Stay Connected

Our FOCUS newsletter keeps you informed about upcoming events, ministry opportunities, special services, and inspiring stories from our church family. Through FOCUS, we aim to strengthen our bonds as a community of believers and keep everyone connected to the life of our church.

Whether you're a long-time member or new to our church family, the FOCUS newsletter is an excellent way to stay engaged with what God is doing in and through The Greater First Baptist Church.

"A cheerful look brings joy to the heart, and good news gives health to the bones."

Proverbs 15:30 (NIV)

Get Involved

If you'd like to contribute to the FOCUS newsletter or have news to share with the church family, please contact the church office. We welcome submissions about ministry activities, personal testimonies, upcoming events, and prayer requests.

To receive the FOCUS newsletter, please ensure the church office has your current contact information. The newsletter is distributed regularly to keep our church family informed and connected.
